Candidate Responsibilities

  • Accurate Information: Provide truthful and complete information in your application.
  • Timely Responses: Respond to communication within 48 hours when possible.
  • Interview Preparation: Prepare adequately for scheduled interviews.
  • Professional Conduct: Maintain professionalism throughout the process.
  • Updates: Inform us of any changes to your availability or job preferences.
  • Feedback: Provide constructive feedback about your experience.

Job Matching Criteria

  • Skills & Experience: Alignment with job requirements.
  • Career Goals: Match with your long-term objectives.
  • Location Preferences: Geographic and remote work options.
  • Salary Expectations: Alignment with compensation ranges.
  • Company Culture: Fit with organizational values and environment.
  • Growth Potential: Opportunities for career advancement.
